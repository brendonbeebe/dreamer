To participate, all you need is an internet connection and the desire to turn your passions into a real business. Sign up now and you’ll get free access to our online training program, AND you’ll get $20 to start your business after completing our videos and quizzes.

After that, you’ll have access to our crowd-funding platform and mentorship program where you can raise money for your idea, and get free advice from successful business mentors.
