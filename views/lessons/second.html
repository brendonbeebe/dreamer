Lesson 2

Finding your passion

Coming up with an idea:

Find something you love and you will never have to work a day in your life.

This statement has never been so true. Teens all over the world are discovering ways to turn their passions into profitable businesses. Whether you are into snowboarding, painting, or cooking, with the proper tools and knowledge you will be able to start your own business.

You should be able to describe your idea in one sentence and a possible solution in another sentence. That’s two sentences.

Do a brain dump:

This is great if you’ve got a few ideas stored in your head somewhere. Write the ideas down. Express each one as a problem and make an attempt at imagining how a website or mobile app might provide a solution.

Become a detective:

In the event that your brain dump doesn’t provide you with any ideas, or if you want to come up with even more ideas, put on your detective cap and start looking for clues. Start with yourself. What bugs you? Why does it bug you? How can you make it better? Next, observe and listen to everyone around you, especially your family and friends. What kinds of problems are they having that you can solve using the web or a mobile app? Write down these problems and possible solutions, too. The more you observe and the more you listen, the more opportunities you will see and create!
